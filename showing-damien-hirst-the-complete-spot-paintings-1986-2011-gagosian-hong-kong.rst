					<h2>Showing: Damien Hirst – “The Complete Spot Paintings 1986-2011″ @ Gagosian (Hong Kong)</h2>					<div class="content">						<p><a href="http://arrestedmotion.com/2012/01/showing-damien-hirst-the-complete-spot-paintings-1986-2011-gagosian-hong-kong/img_39821/" rel="attachment wp-att-163162"><img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-163162" height="956" src="http://arrestedmotion.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_39821.jpg" title="IMG_3982(1)" width="1280" /></a></p><p>Next stop on our tour of <a href="http://www.damienhirst.com/" target="_blank"><strong>Damien Hirst&#8217;s</strong></a> <em>The Complete Spot Paintings 1986-2011</em> after leaving his <a href="http://arrestedmotion.com/2012/01/openings-damien-hirst-spot-paintings-the-gagosian-paris/" target="_blank"><strong>Paris showing</strong></a> takes us to the <a href="http://www.gagosian.com/" target="_blank"><strong>Gagosian</strong></a> in Hong Kong. The most prominent member of the group known as the <strong><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Young_British_Artists" title="Young British Artists">Young British Artists</a></strong> certainly has outdone himself with this current slate of exhibitions (at least in terms of scope and ambition). This lone display in Asia of his famous dotted motif is no different than the other eleven simultaneous shows planned &#8211; filled with paintings, spots, and canvases of different sizes, colors, shapes, and numbers.</p><p>More photos by <span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong><a href="http://arrestedmotion.com" target="_blank"><span style="color: #ff0000;">AM</span></a></strong></span>
